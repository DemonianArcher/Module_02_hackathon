    Draft overview:
    Team One (Win): Orianna (Mid), Kai’Sa (ADC), Leona (Support), Graves (Jungle), Malphite (Top)
    Team Two (Loss): Viktor (Mid), Jhin (ADC), Thresh (Support), Viego (Jungle), Camille (Top)
    Team One Strategy: Hard engage + AoE wombo combo (Malphite–Orianna–Kai’Sa).
    Team Two Strategy: Pick comp with Thresh hooks and Camille dives, backed by Viktor zone control.

    Game Flow:
    Early Game (0–12 min): Ironclad led with pressure — Camille secured first blood top, and Viego picked up two kills around Dragon. They secured the first two Drakes uncontested.
    Mid Game (12–22 min): Eclipse held firm, trading objectives for farm. Orianna scaled up, and Kai’Sa quietly caught up in CS despite Jhin’s lane dominance.
    Turning Point (23 min): At the third Dragon, Ironclad tried to force a pick with Thresh. Malphite counter-engaged with a 3-man Unstoppable Force, followed by Orianna’s Shockwave. Eclipse wiped Ironclad 5–1 and claimed Baron.
    Late Game (24–29 min): With Baron buff and superior teamfight scaling, Eclipse pushed through mid. A second wombo combo near inhibitor sealed the game in under 30 minutes.
